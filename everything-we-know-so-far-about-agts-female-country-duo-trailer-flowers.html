    Trailer Flowers Are Nashville-Based Best Friends-Turned-Band

    Battling their struggles amid quarantine, Jack and Brooke alongside their other best friends have decided to transform letdowns into creativity. Trailer Flowers have been making songs and performing together since 2020. Best recognized for their “tight harmonies and transparency,” the country duo have graced private parties, house shows, listening rooms, and breweries.

    “Authenticity is the most important staple to their country, modern, hippy, western sound,” their website bio reads. “Their faith, and desire to love well is expressed in the bangers and the ballads found in their energetic live show filling up a room with sound, just their voices and a guitar.”

    At present, Trailer Flowers are pursuing music while also working as service crews. According to their website, Jack and Brooke are trying to quit their server jobs and go after their musical ambitions. 

    Trailer Flowers believe that they can “make good things out of sh*t.” With that mantra, creating music serves as their way to celebrate their broken parts.

    They Are Competing on America’s Got Talent 2023

    Last June 5, Trailer Flowers revealed on Instagram that they’ve made a big step forward towards their dreams. Apparently, the country duo took the shot and joined America’s Got Talent this year.

    “This is the official announcement that your girls who you have been loving on & cheering for have scored a major win! We got to audition for America’s Got Talent,” they wrote.

    In the same post, Trailer Flowers reflected on this venture, saying that AGT might be their chance to be big in the industry. As they stated, the decision to join did not happen without sacrifice.

    “We have been recording music, making moves all while still hustling at the bar,” they wrote. “We believe we are very close to getting the full package dream of creating full time & connecting with souls we love.”

    Trailer Flowers’ audition performance will air soon. If you want to witness how the duo does, be sure to catch America’s Got Talent on Tuesday on NBC.
