    The America’s Got Talent stage is a perfect place for comedians to debut themselves to the country. Although a comedian has never won AGT, many have rose to the fame following their appearance on the national stage.

    The Best Comedy Auditions on AGT 2023

    1. Orlando Leyba

    Starting out the season on the right note was Orlando Leyba. Although he was the first comedian of the season, his anecdotes about a vacation in Yellowstone National Park impressed the judges. Orlando earned four yeses from the judges.

    2. Mandy Muden

    Although Mandy Muden was technically a magician, she deserved to be added to the comedian list, since she’s also a comedian. The comedy magic act included Mandy making sexually suggestive jokes to each of the judges. Mandy earned four yeses from the judges.

    3. Ahren Belisle

    Ahren Belisle is one of the most unique comedians that has ever been on AGT. The comedian came to the stage, making jokes about his cerebral palsy using his phone text-to-speech. Ahren earned four yeses from the judges.

    4. Riccardo Pace

    Technically, Riccardo Pace wasn’t intended to be a comedy act, but he deserves to be on this list. After coming to the stage completely serious, Riccardo showed off his impressive hand farting. Simon Cowell voted “no,” while Sofia Vergara, Heidi Klum, and Howie Mandel voted “yes.”

    5. Maureen Langan

    Comedian Maureen Langan came to the AGT stage performing a stand-up routine about how she hated people, and how she wants an elderly partner. Judges loved this performance, voting her through to the next round.

    6. Barry Brewer Jr.

    Barry Brewer Jr is a unique comedian in the way that he combines both jokes and music. While playing the piano, he performed a stand up comedy routine about his journey to America and the difference between Black & White church music. The judges love this act, voting him through to the next round.
