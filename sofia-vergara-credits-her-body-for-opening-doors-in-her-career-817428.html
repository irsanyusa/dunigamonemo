    Despite her recent divorce from Joe Manganiello, Sofía Vergara’s career is doing better than ever. The 51-year-old Hollywood star has just released a new miniseries. In a fresh interview about her character, Griselda, Vergara also opened up about her professional journey, and how her shapely figure helped her jumpstart her career.

    Modern Family star Sofía Vergara opened up about her career in a new interview.

    In a recent interview with El País, Sofía Vergara talked about her new miniseries, Griselda. At the same time, the 51-year-old bombshell had the chance to discuss her career and give more details about the beginnings of her successful modelling and acting journey.

    Vergara admitted that her looks helped her a lot when she was just starting out.

    Actress Sofía Vergara recently shared insights into her professional journey, emphasizing the important role her remarkable physique has played in opening doors throughout her career. Reflecting on the impact of her figure on her Hollywood trajectory, Vergara expressed gratitude for the opportunities it has brought her way. She acknowledged the influence of her looks, stating that denying it would be absurd, and instead, she embraces her life with appreciation.

    “My giant boobs and my body opened doors for me; they were my passport to the world when I was 20 years old, when I started as a model, but today I’m 51-years-old, and I’m still here,” the famous actress confessed.

    At the same time, a lot of hard work went into achieving great success.

    Even though she has always been aware of the effect her physical appearance had on people, Vergara knows that a lot of perseverance was also needed for achieving her goals. “When I was very young, since high school the boys have all wanted to be with me. Not that it made me feel any better, but you realize that they treat you differently. From a very young age, I’ve known my strengths, and I’ve played them, but if you only see my boobs, then that’s your problem.”

    Now at 51, the actress is very satisfied with her career.

    At the age of 51, Sofía Vergara is currently experiencing unprecedented fame and success. Expressing a profound sense of fulfillment, she acknowledges surpassing her own career aspirations, having achieved far more than she ever dared to dream. Although her beauty jumpstarted her career, the actress is aware that wisdom and talent were also needed for maintaining her success.

    “I am not afraid of risks, I work harder than anyone, I have the personality, I have always been aware of what was outside, and I have not been afraid. There are women who are prettier, younger and who have bigger breasts and a better body than me, but I’m still around because I have demonstrated that I can stay.”
