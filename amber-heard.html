    Profession: Actress

    Biography: Amber Heard is an American actress, having played supporting roles in a number of major films including Pineapple Express (2008) and Magic Mike XXL (2015). She is also a part of the DC Extended Universe, particularly having played Mera in the Aquaman series of films.

    Heard has recently been in the spotlight for her marriage to Johnny Depp in 2015, and the dissolution of their marriage in 2016. The end of their relationship turned into a sprawling media and legal fiasco, with a Washington Post Op-Ed written by Heard accusing Depp of domestic abuse serving as the basis for a defamation lawsuit against her by Depp.
