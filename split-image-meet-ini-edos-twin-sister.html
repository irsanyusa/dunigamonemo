    While Ini Edo is from Onna Local Government Area in Akwa Ibom State, South-South Nigeria, her twin sister is called Esther Urrah, a Benue State University graduate from Oturkpo, in that state.

    The young lady who is a split image of Ini Edo, said she was not aware of the striking resemblance with her idol until some friends pointed it out to her.

    Her life ambition now is to meet the super actress and if possible, play a movie role beside her.
