    Meet Alexandr Leshchenko, the captivating Ukrainian dancer and choreographer ready to dazzle audiences with his 3D dance performance on season 18 of America’s Got Talent. With an illustrious career that spans several countries and prestigious dance shows, Alexandr has established himself as one of the most sought-after dancers in Ukraine.

    Who is Alexandr Leshchenko?

    From an early age, Alexandr displayed a natural talent and passion for dance. Over the years, he honed his skills and graced stages across the globe, captivating audiences in Russia, France, Ukraine, Germany, and the United States.

    In 2021, Alexandr participated in Ukraine’s Got Talent, where he mesmerized both the judges and viewers with his extraordinary 3D show choreography. His unique and unconventional approach to dance earned him a well-deserved place in the finals.

    Alexandr’s talent and creativity also shone through on various popular Ukrainian dance television projects. He claimed an impressive second place in the debut season of Everybody Dance! – Ukraine’s version of So You Think You Can Dance. This accomplishment catapulted his career, opening doors to new opportunities and collaborations.

    In addition, Alexandr served as the chief choreographer of the television shows Superstar and the second season of Maidan’s. His exceptional vision and ability to infuse innovative dance routines into these productions further showed his versatility as a choreographer and director.

    He Co-Founded The Foresight Art Group

    In 2006, Alexandr co-founded the Foresight Art Group alongside director Lina Veres. The Foresight Ballet, a key component of the group, has since become the most acclaimed and beloved ballet company in Ukraine. With their one of a kind performances, they have been awarded “The Ballet of the Year” for three consecutive years from 2010 to 2012.

    The Foresight Art Group encompasses a wide range of creative services, including production shows, performance writing, screenplay development, and event management. Under Alexandr’s direction, the group has organized awe-inspiring events such as the prestigious Awards Ceremony “Teletriumph,” the esteemed music award “YUNA,” and the pioneering First World PoleArtShow.

    Having conquered the stages of Ukraine, Alexandr is now poised to compete against the world’s finest talents on the grand stage of AGT. With his distinctive style and masterful dance moves, he is undoubtedly a top contender in season 18 of the show.
