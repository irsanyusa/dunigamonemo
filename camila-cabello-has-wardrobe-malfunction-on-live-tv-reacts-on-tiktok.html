    Camila Cabello had a wardrobe malfunction on live TV this week while promoting her new collaboration with Ed Sheeran, “Bam Bam.” The former The Voice Battle Advisor later reacted to the embarrassing moment with a funny TikTok video.

    Camila Cabello Reacts to Wardrobe Malfunction on TikTok

    Cabello appeared via video chat on BBC’s The One Show to talk about her new single. After being asked to share her favorite dance move from the song’s music video, the singer stood up to demonstrate. While adjusting her shirt, she briefly flashed her nipple to the camera.

    “I flashed you … I hope you didn’t see nipple,” she said afterward.

    The hosts were nice about the moment, with Alex Jones saying, “It happens to the best of us, doesn’t it?” Co-host Jermaine Jenas said of Cabello, “She dealt with it brilliantly.”

    Cabello later took to TikTok to address the incident. She captioned the video, “when my stylist asked me if I wanted nip covers and I said no.” In the clip, she lip syncs to the lyrics, “I wish I had a time machine.”

    “Least they’re cute tho,” she added in a comment.

    SEE ALSO: ‘THE VOICE’ MENTORS CAMILA CABELLO, ED SHEERAN DROP NEW SONG ‘BAM BAM’

    Fans Are Loving Cabello’s New Song ‘Bam Bam’

    Billboard reports that fans voted Cabello’s new song “Bam Bam” with Ed Sheeran to be their favorite new music release last week, with more than 76 percent of the vote. The singer released the track last Friday, along with a music video.

    “Bam Bam” will appear on Cabello’s upcoming third solo album, titled Familia. The album will be out on April 8, and will also feature the previously released single “Don’t Go Yet,” as well as “La Buena Vida.”

    Last season on The Voice, Cabello appeared as a Battle Advisor for John Legend’s team. Later this year, she’ll be opening for Coldplay on the Latin American leg of their Music of the Spheres World Tour.
