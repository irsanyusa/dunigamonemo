    Checking examination performance can cause anxiety. If you are confident that you did your best, you should familiarise yourself with the steps you should follow to check your JAMB results.

    How do you check your JAMB result?

    All candidates who sat their UTME examinations will access their scores using the JAMB result checker number issued by the board. You can check your scores using different methods, as explored below.

    How to check your JAMB results via SMS

    Follow the steps below to discover how you can see your scores via SMS.

    • On your phone, go to messages and start a new message.
    • Type UTMERESULT and send it to 55019. You must send the message using the same number you used to register for the UTME examination.
    • A message with your scores will be sent to your phone.

    NB: You must only use the JAMB result checker number given to access details of your performance. Only one number has been issued by the board, so candidates should be cautious because fraudsters tend to send messages with misleading information.

    How to check your results on the JAMB portal

    If you have been wondering how to check your JAMB results with your registration number, read on. Note that the official JAMB website is the main platform candidates should use to access their UTME results, besides SMS.

    The JAMB results portal allows candidates to print their results slips. Here are the steps you should follow to access the results.

    • Open a web browser on either your phone or laptop and visit the JAMBâ€™s eFacility portal.
    • Enter your login details: Here, you will need to provide the email address and password you used during registration.
    • Access your results: Once you have entered the required details, click the Check UTME Results button.
    • Your UTME score for each subject tested should be displayed on the screen if it has been released. You can save the results to print later. If your device is connected to a printer, you can print the results immediately.
