    John Legend’s new album is out on June 19th. But if you follow Chrissy Teigen on Instagram, you can probably hear the full album for free a lot sooner than you think. Chrissy posted a video listening to one of John’s new songs and his hilarious response is something you have to see.

    Chrissy Teigen Posted The Song Leak Because She Was Mad

    According to her Instagram caption, Chrissy said that she would continue to leak John’s album when she gets “mad enough.” While she could totally be trolling everyone and could’ve planned this whole thing, it is pretty funny that she has this kind of relationship with her husband. In the short video, Chrissy is bopping her head along to a new song and she looks super into it. From the quick teaser, it sounds like it really will be John’s next big hit. She also posted the same video on Twitter with the caption “really just trying to piss him off here.”

    However, it seems like her tactic did not work because John commented “I call this free promotion. Leak on.” He is in complete support of his wife leaking his music to her 29.8 million followers but hey, maybe it will get more people to stream his new album when it comes out. She also took to her Instagram stories to post an adorable video that will literally make you say “aw.” In the video, John is dancing along to his new song with children Miles and Luna. Chrissy is laughing from behind the camera as she captures the heartfelt moment.

    SEE ALSO: JOHN LEGEND CAN’T WAIT TO EAT OUT AGAIN DESPITE CHRISSY’S HOME COOKING

    Chrissy Tweeted About Some Questionable Lines In John’s New Music

    The Twitter queen told her followers that she was listening to the new album for the first time. But, there was one song in particular that had her questioning her husband. The line in question is “You know there’s a lot of women tryin to take your place” to which Chrissy questioned by saying “b*tch what the f*ck?” John replied to the tweet saying that there is a punchline “But I’m focused. I’m focused on you.” He clearly has all the love in the world for his wife.

    To throw some shade at John, she had a hilarious response. “This is why I’m gonna keep my options open with the men of Better Call Saul,” she tweeted. She previously tweeted that she was binge-watching the AMC show and said she was “loving the hot drug guy who also looks like Stitch.” Another thing that caught Chrissy’s attention on the new album is a collab with artist Jhene Aiko. She said “One of my favorite ones is one with @JheneAiko why don’t u guys just get f*ckin married!!!” Chrissy is obviously kidding and how else would we get through our days without a little bit of sarcasm from her.

    Regardless of the outrageous things Chrissy says on social media, it does seem like John is totally cool with it. He already dropped one song from the album with an amazing music video. The title track from his album “Bigger Love” features not only fan videos but also several cameos from Chrissy and the kids.

    Definitely be on the lookout for more Instagram stories from Chrissy Teigen giving glimpses of John’s new music.
