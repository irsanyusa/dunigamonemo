The owners, the Goldman Sachs Trading Company, wanted to see how the public would take to this novel idea of offering food for sale. Doctors Think a Woman's Tattoo Caused Her Severe Leg Pain

22 May 2024, 00:00

Doctors in Scotland believe a woman’s large tattoo on her leg may have been the cause of her unexplained inflammation and severe leg pain. The authors of a report on the woman’s case, published in BMJ Case Reports, say it is a reminder to physicians that they should be mindful of their patients’ tattoos if they encounter unusual and unexplained symptoms. The 31-year-old woman was suffering a condition called inflammatory myopathy, which affected her left leg and caused pain severe enough to disrupt her sleep.
