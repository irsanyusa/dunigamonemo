    Unlike the original Avatar, where actors like Sam Worthington, Stephen Lang, and Sigourney Weaver were prominently featured in their own bodies, Avatar: The Way of Water shows the cast as avatars for the majority of its 3-hour runtime. That’s why we decided to uncover the actors who brought the characters of Pandora to life by finding out who is hiding behind the special effects.

    1. Jake Sully is portrayed by Sam Worthington.

    2. Neytiri, Jake Sully’s wife, is portrayed by Zoe Saldaña.

    3. Neteyam, Jake and Neytiri’s oldest son, is portrayed by Jamie Flatters.

    4. Kiri, Jake and Neytiri’s adopted daughter, is portrayed by Sigourney Weaver.

    5. Lo’ak, Jake and Neytiri’s second son, is portrayed by Britain Dalton.

    6. Tuk, Jake and Neytiri’s youngest child, is portrayed by Trinity Jo-Li Bliss.

    7. Miles, also known as Spider, Colonel Miles Quaritch’s son, is portrayed by Jack Champion.

    8. Ronal, the matriarch of a water clan, is portrayed by Kate Winslet.

    9. Tonowari, the leader of the Mekayina clan, is portrayed by Cliff Curtis.

    10. Tsireya, Ronal and Tonowari’s daughter, is portrayed by Bailey Bass.

    11. Mo’at, the clan’s spiritual leader and Neytiri’s mother, is portrayed by CCH Pounder.

    12. Colonel Miles Quaritch is portrayed by Stephen Lang.
