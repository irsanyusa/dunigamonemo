    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Profession: Actor and Comedian

    Biography: Rose to fame with his role as the alien Mork in the TV series "Mork & Mindy", he went on to establish a successful career in both stand-up comedy and as a critically acclaimed actor.

    Williams received the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his performance in "Good Will Hunting".

    He also received two Primetime Emmy Awards, four Golden Globes, two Screen Actors Guild Awards and five Grammy Awards.

    Born: July 21, 1951
    Birthplace: Chicago, Illinois, USA

    Generation: Baby Boomer
    Chinese Zodiac: Rabbit
    Star Sign: Cancer

    Died: August 11, 2014 (aged 63)
    Cause of Death: Committed suicide by hanging himself
